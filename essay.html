About Me

My name is Kira Hawkins. I am from Stafford, Virginia, but I have spent the last four years in Provo, Utah earning my bachelor's degree from Brigham Young University (BYU) in Exercise & Wellness with a minor in Business. Growing up I played soccer and took ballet lessons. During high school, I started running track and cross country. My freshman year at BYU I competed for the school as a walk-on non-scholarship athlete.

My previous volunteer and work experience is varied. I worked as a cashier, host, and trainer at the Cannon Center, which is BYU’s freshman dining hall. I worked as a lifeguard at my local YMCA and have an active certification for lifeguarding (including deep water) with CPR and AED for professional rescuers and first aid. I have shadowed Chiropractors and Massage Therapists at Ultimate Family Wellness, which provided hands-on massage experience. In my current position with BYU Purchasing & Travel, I work as an expense report specialist under the direct billing team. I am also currently working on the internship marketing team for a business called Warrior Rising. Lastly, I participated in an internship with BYU’s Strength & Conditioning program where I worked on a 12-week training program and coached the collegiate athletes in the weight room.

Academically I am very successful. I graduated high school with an advanced diploma as well as the International Baccalaureate diploma. I will graduate with my undergraduate degree at the end of April. Earning my undergraduate degree has given me great experience in athletic training. The basic athletic training class at BYU had two parts, a lecture and a taping lab. I really enjoyed taking what I had learned in anatomy, physiology, and my athletic training lecture and applying it to the lab. During and after the class, I have been able to provide aid for my friends and family when they have a minor injury. I loved using my newfound skills to serve my community and I hope to learn even more so I can turn it into a career.

Outside of academics and work, I like to read and spend time with my friends and family. I would describe myself as an extremely hard worker with goals and high expectations. I have learned how to be a leader and I respect my teammates, peers, and authority figures. I have a positive attitude even when things are challenging and I like to be organized in my work. I have always been a high achiever and I will carry those skills throughout the rest of my life.
